Fits 3 CTF parameters to a transverse section of focal series power spectra.

    constant phase shift (amplitude contrast)
    defocus

Reconstructs the exit wave from a focal series.

Parameters
*pfocal series (images or transforms).
cpCTF parameters with average defocus.
hireshigh resolution limit.
max_itermaximum number of iterations.
toltolerance: minimum change to accept to set a break point.
ctf_flagCTF form: 0=complex, 1=sine, 2=envelope, 4=combined.
exit_flagreference images: 0=intensities, 1=sqrt(int), 2=real.
Returns
Bimage* resultant complex image. 
The input must be a real space stack of images.
Reference: Allen et al. Ultramicroscopy 100 (2004) 91104.
